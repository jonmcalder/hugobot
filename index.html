Hugo is a chat bot built on the Hubot framework. He was initially generated by generator-hubot, and configured to be deployed on Heroku.

If you're looking to setup your own instance of hubot then explore the above links and/or have a look at my blog post on Hugo which provides an overview of the setup process.
